# Dark Mode

## Visual Identity
Sleek, modern, and easy on the eyes. Dark backgrounds with high-contrast light text create a premium, focused experience. Reduces eye strain and makes accent colors pop dramatically.

## Color Palette
- **Background:** Near-black (`#111111`, `#1A1A1A`, `#0D0D0D`) or dark charcoal (`#222222`)
- **Text:** White (`#FFFFFF`), light gray (`#E0E0E0`, `#B0B0B0`)
- **Accent:** Any saturated color pops on dark — gold (`#FFD700`), electric blue (`#3B82F6`), crimson (`#EF4444`), emerald (`#10B981`)
- **Avoid:** Mid-tone grays that wash out; low-contrast text colors

## Typography
- **Font:** Clean, modern sans-serif — Inter, SF Pro, Manrope, Space Grotesk
- **Font weight:** Regular (400) for body, semi-bold (600) for headings
- **Font size:** Slightly larger than light mode equivalents for readability
- **Font color:** White (`#FFFFFF`) or off-white (`#F5F5F5`)

## Background
- **Type:** Solid dark (`bg_type: 0`) — most common. Subtle dark gradient (`bg_type: 1`) for depth
- **Gradient:** `#1A1A2E` → `#16213E` (deep blue-black)
- **Image (optional):** Dark-tinted photography, subtle noise texture
- **Padding:** 12-16px; comfortable spacing

## Borders & Radius
- **Border:** Subtle 1px in dark gray (`#333333`) — `border_style: 1`
- **Radius:** `4px` to `8px` — modern rounded
- **Avoid:** Bright borders unless intentional accent

## CTA / Button
- **Style:** Solid (`btn_style: 0` or `2`) with bright accent color
- **Color:** White text on accent background
- **Text:** Clean and direct — "Shop Now", "Get Started", "Learn More"

## Coupon/Discount
- **Style:** Outlined (`discount_style.border: 1` or `3`) with accent color border
- **Background:** Semi-transparent accent (e.g., `rgba(59, 130, 246, 0.15)`)
- **Animation:** None (`animation: 0`); keeps it sleek
- **Text:** Light color matching the theme

## When to Use
- Tech products, gaming, streaming services, night-owl brands
- User says "dark", "dark mode", "night", "black background", "dark theme"
- Stores with a dark brand identity or dark-themed website

## When NOT to Use
- Brands with predominantly light/white website themes (may clash)
- Kid-oriented stores (use `playful_fun`)
- Outdoor/daylight-themed products (use `light_mode` or `modern_clean`)

## Example
> "Dark mode banner for our tech accessory store — #1A1A1A background, white Inter text, electric blue (#3B82F6) CTA button."
