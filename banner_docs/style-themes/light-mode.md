# Light Mode

## Visual Identity
Airy, bright, and approachable. White or near-white backgrounds with clean dark text create an open, trustworthy feel. The digital equivalent of a well-lit room — easy to read, universally accessible.

## Color Palette
- **Background:** Pure white (`#FFFFFF`), off-white (`#FAFAFA`), very light gray (`#F5F5F5`)
- **Text:** Dark charcoal (`#1A1A1A`), dark gray (`#333333`), or deep navy (`#1E293B`)
- **Accent:** Soft, approachable — sky blue (`#60A5FA`), sage green (`#86B5A0`), warm coral (`#FB923C`)
- **Avoid:** Pure black text (too harsh); dark backgrounds; overly saturated accents

## Typography
- **Font:** Clean, readable sans-serif — Inter, DM Sans, Nunito, Open Sans
- **Font weight:** Regular (400) for body; medium (500) for emphasis
- **Font size:** Standard readability — 14-16px body
- **Font color:** Dark but not pure black (`#1A1A1A` or `#333333`)

## Background
- **Type:** Solid (`bg_type: 0`) — almost always
- **Gradient:** Subtle at most — e.g., `#FFFFFF` → `#F8FAFC`
- **Image (optional):** Light, airy photography with low opacity overlay
- **Padding:** 12-16px; balanced breathing room

## Borders & Radius
- **Border:** Subtle 1px (`#E5E7EB`) — `border_style: 1` — or none
- **Radius:** `4px` to `8px` for a soft, approachable feel
- **Avoid:** Thick borders, harsh colors

## CTA / Button
- **Style:** Rounded solid (`btn_style: 2` or `4`) with soft accent color
- **Color:** White text on a medium-saturation accent background
- **Text:** Friendly and clear — "Learn More", "Shop Now", "Get Started"

## Coupon/Discount
- **Style:** Light outlined (`discount_style.border: 3`) with accent border
- **Background:** Very light tint of accent (5-10% opacity)
- **Animation:** None (`animation: 0`)
- **Text:** Readable dark color

## When to Use
- Blogs, lifestyle brands, wellness, skincare, home decor, general e-commerce
- User says "light", "white background", "bright", "airy", "clean"
- Brands wanting maximum readability and accessibility
- Sites with light-themed design systems

## When NOT to Use
- Gaming or nightlife brands (use `dark_mode` or `cyberpunk_futuristic`)
- Urgent flash sales (use `bold_urgent`)
- High-drama campaigns (use `bold_urgent` or `dark_mode`)
