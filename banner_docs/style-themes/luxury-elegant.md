# Luxury & Elegant

## Visual Identity
Refined, prestigious, and aspirational. Every detail conveys quality. Muted, sophisticated palettes with metallic accents. Generous whitespace and premium typography signal exclusivity and craftsmanship.

## Color Palette
- **Background:** Black (`#000000`), deep charcoal (`#1A1A1A`), cream (`#FFFDD0`), or ivory (`#FFFFF0`)
- **Text:** Gold (`#D4AF37`), champagne (`#F7E7CE`), silver (`#C0C0C0`), or crisp white
- **Accent:** Gold (`#D4AF37`), rose gold (`#B76E79`), platinum (`#E5E4E2`)
- **Avoid:** Bright/saturated colors, neons, rainbow palettes, multiple competing hues

## Typography
- **Font:** Elegant serif â€” Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, Bodoni Moda, Cinzel, Lora
- **Font weight:** Light (300) for refined body text; regular (400) for headings
- **Font size:** Generous letter-spacing (tracking); capitalized headings create formality
- **Use Google Fonts:** Always provide `font_url` for luxury serif fonts

## Background
- **Type:** Dark solid (`bg_type: 0`) for high-end fashion; cream solid for jewelry
- **Gradient:** Subtle metallic â€” e.g., `#1A1A1A` â†’ `#2D2D2D` (dark luxury)
- **Image (optional):** Marble texture, silk fabric, subtle gold foil pattern
- **Padding:** Generous â€” 16-20px; luxury needs breathing room

## Borders & Radius
- **Border:** Thin gold/silver (`border_style: 1`), `1px` width, or none
- **Radius:** Sharp (`0px`) or barely rounded (`2px`); sophistication over trendiness
- **Avoid:** Thick borders, fully rounded corners, playful outlines

## CTA / Button
- **Style:** Solid minimalist (`btn_style: 0`) or elegant outlined (`btn_style: 1`)
- **Color:** Gold or white background with dark text; or dark button with gold text
- **Text:** Elegant and understated â€” "Discover", "Explore the Collection", "Shop Now"
- **Animation:** None (`animation: 0`); animations cheapen the luxury feel

## Coupon/Discount (rarely used)
- **Style:** Minimal outlined (`discount_style.border: 1`)
- **Background:** Transparent or subtle gold tint
- **Text:** Gold or white; no animations
- **Note:** Luxury brands rarely use visible coupon codes â€” prefer exclusive/silent discounts

## When to Use
- Luxury fashion, fine jewelry, high-end watches, premium beauty, exclusive memberships
- User says "luxury", "premium", "high-end", "elegant", "sophisticated", "exclusive"
- Brands with AOV > $200, heritage brands, designer labels

## When NOT to Use
- Discount/dollar stores (use `bold_urgent`)
- Casual/fast fashion (use `modern_clean` or `playful_fun`)
- Flash sales (use `bold_urgent`)
