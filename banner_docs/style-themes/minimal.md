# Minimal

## Visual Identity
Clean, uncluttered, and purposeful. Every element earns its place. Heavy use of whitespace, subtle typography, and restrained color accents. The banner feels lightweight and lets the message breathe.

## Color Palette
- **Background:** White (`#FFFFFF`), off-white (`#F9F9F9`), or very light neutral (`#F5F5F5`)
- **Text:** Dark gray (`#333333`) or black (`#000000`)
- **Accent:** A single muted accent color used sparingly — e.g., soft blue (`#4A90D9`), muted green (`#5B8C5A`)
- **Avoid:** Gradients, bright colors, multiple competing hues

## Typography
- **Font:** Clean sans-serif — Inter, Helvetica Neue, SF Pro, Roboto
- **Font weight:** Light or regular for body; medium for emphasis
- **Font size:** Moderate, never oversized. Leave generous letter-spacing

## Background
- **Type:** Solid color (`bg_type: 0`), never gradient or image
- **Opacity:** 100%
- **Padding:** Generous — minimum 14px top/bottom, 16px left/right

## Borders & Radius
- **Border:** None (`border_style: 0`) or subtle 1px solid in a light gray
- **Radius:** `0px` (sharp) or `4px` (barely rounded)
- **Avoid:** Thick borders, glowing effects, strong border colors

## CTA / Button
- **Style:** Outlined (`btn_style: 1` or `3`) or minimal text link
- **Color:** Matches accent color; transparent background
- **Text:** Short and action-oriented — "Shop", "Learn more", "→"

## Coupon/Discount
- **Style:** Plain text or subtle dashed border (`discount_style.border: 6`)
- **Background:** Transparent or very light accent tint
- **Animation:** None (`animation: 0`)

## When to Use
- Modern D2C brands, SaaS, tech companies, design-focused stores
- User explicitly asks for "minimal", "clean", "simple", "no clutter"
- Banner should not compete with above-the-fold hero content

## When NOT to Use
- Flash sales or urgency-driven campaigns (use `bold_urgent`)
- Seasonal/holiday promotions (use `seasonal_holiday`)
- Kid-oriented or toy stores (use `playful_fun`)

## Example
> "Create a minimal banner announcing our new collection — clean white background, dark text, small 'Shop Now' link."
