# Modern & Clean

## Visual Identity
Polished, professional, and balanced. A contemporary look that instills trust without being flashy. Neutral backgrounds with a single brand-aligned accent color. Everything feels intentional and well-proportioned.

## Color Palette
- **Background:** White (`#FFFFFF`), soft gray (`#F0F2F5`), or a subtle brand tint
- **Text:** Dark charcoal (`#1A1A1A`) or deep navy (`#1E293B`)
- **Accent:** Brand primary color — blues (`#2563EB`), teals (`#0D9488`), or warm coral (`#F97316`)
- **Avoid:** More than 2 accent colors; neon or overly saturated hues

## Typography
- **Font:** Modern sans-serif — Inter, Plus Jakarta Sans, DM Sans, Satoshi
- **Font weight:** Regular for body (400), semi-bold for headings (600)
- **Font size:** Well-proportioned hierarchy — 14-16px body

## Background
- **Type:** Solid color (`bg_type: 0`) primarily; subtle gradient (`bg_type: 1`) acceptable for brand flair
- **Gradient:** Two closely-related tones, e.g., `#6366F1` → `#818CF8`
- **Padding:** 12-16px consistently

## Borders & Radius
- **Border:** None or 1px subtle (`#E5E7EB`)
- **Radius:** `4px` or `8px` — subtle rounding
- **Style:** `border_style: 0` (none) or `2` (rounded solid)

## CTA / Button
- **Style:** Rounded solid (`btn_style: 2` or `4`) with brand accent color
- **Text:** White or high-contrast on the button background
- **Content:** Clear call to action — "Get Started", "Shop Now", "Learn More"

## Coupon/Discount
- **Style:** Rounded outlined (`discount_style.border: 2` or `4`)
- **Background:** Light tint of accent color (e.g., 10% opacity)
- **Animation:** None or subtle (`animation: 0`)

## When to Use
- Corporate, SaaS, fintech, professional services, B2B brands
- User says "modern", "professional", "corporate", "clean"
- E-commerce brands wanting a trustworthy, conversion-optimized feel

## When NOT to Use
- Artistic/creative portfolios (may feel too corporate)
- Gaming or entertainment brands (use `cyberpunk_futuristic` or `playful_fun`)
- Budget/discount-heavy stores (use `bold_urgent`)
