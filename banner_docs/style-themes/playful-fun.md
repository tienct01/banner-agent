# Playful & Fun

## Visual Identity
Bright, energetic, and smile-inducing. Bold colors, chunky typography, bouncy animations, and rounded everything. Feels like a candy store, a cartoon, or a celebration. Designed to delight.

## Color Palette
- **Background:** Bright yellow (`#FFD700`), sky blue (`#87CEEB`), hot pink (`#FF69B4`), lime green (`#32CD32`), or rainbow gradient
- **Text:** Dark but friendly — deep purple (`#4A0E5C`), dark navy (`#1A237E`), or white on dark backgrounds
- **Accent:** Any bright, saturated color; don't limit to one — playful themes can handle 2-3 colors
- **Avoid:** Gray, beige, muted tones, pure black

## Typography
- **Font:** Rounded, chunky, or hand-drawn — Fredoka One, Baloo 2, Comic Neue, Luckiest Guy, Nunito
- **Font weight:** Bold (700+) for maximum impact
- **Font size:** Large and expressive; oversized for key words

## Background
- **Type:** Solid (`bg_type: 0`) or gradient (`bg_type: 1`)
- **Gradient:** Bright color to bright color — e.g., `#FF6B6B` → `#FFD93D`
- **Pattern (optional):** Polka dots, confetti, squiggles via background image (`bg_type: 2`)
- **Padding:** Generous (14-18px); room for playful elements

## Borders & Radius
- **Border:** Thick and fun (`border_style: 1` or `2`), bright colored
- **Radius:** Fully rounded (`99px`) — `border_radius: "99px 99px 99px 99px"`
- **Avoid:** Sharp corners, thin/formal borders

## CTA / Button
- **Style:** Fully rounded solid (`btn_style: 4`) with a bright, contrasting color
- **Animation:** Wobble or pulse (`button_styles.animation: 5` or `4`)
- **Text:** Emoji-friendly, enthusiastic — "🎉 Let's Go!", "Grab Yours!", "Yes Please!"

## Coupon/Discount
- **Style:** Rounded solid (`discount_style.border: 2` or `4`)
- **Background:** Bright accent (e.g., hot pink)
- **Animation:** Wobble (`animation: 5`) or color flip (`animation: 1`)
- **Text:** Large, bold, fun

## When to Use
- Kids' products, toy stores, candy/snacks, party supplies, casual apparel
- User says "fun", "playful", "kids", "colorful", "cheerful", "casual", "cute"
- Birthday sales, summer fun campaigns, product launches targeting younger audiences

## When NOT to Use
- Luxury/premium brands (use `luxury_elegant`)
- Professional B2B (use `modern_clean`)
- Serious/urgent announcements (use `bold_urgent`)

## Example
> "Fun banner for our summer toy sale — rainbow gradient background, Fredoka One font, bouncy '🎈 Shop the Fun!' CTA with wobble animation."
