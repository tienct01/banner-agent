# Seasonal & Holiday

## Visual Identity
Thematic and occasion-specific. Each holiday or season has its own visual language — from cozy winter warmth to vibrant summer energy. The banner adapts its color story, imagery, and tone to match the calendar.

## Color Palette (by season/holiday)

| Occasion | Background | Text | Accent |
|----------|-----------|------|--------|
| **Christmas** | Deep red (`#C41E3A`), forest green (`#228B22`), white | White, gold | Gold (`#FFD700`) |
| **Black Friday** | Black (`#000000`), dark gray | White | Red (`#FF0000`), gold |
| **Halloween** | Dark purple (`#2D1B69`), black | Orange, white | Orange (`#FF6B00`), lime |
| **Valentine's** | Soft pink (`#FFB6C1`), white | Deep red | Red (`#E63946`), rose gold |
| **New Year** | Black, dark navy | Gold, white | Gold (`#FFD700`), silver |
| **Spring** | Pastel pink (`#FFD1DC`), mint (`#B2F0C8`) | Dark green | Coral (`#FF7F50`) |
| **Summer** | Bright yellow, sky blue | White, dark blue | Orange (`#FF8C00`) |
| **Autumn/Fall** | Warm orange (`#D2691E`), burgundy | Cream | Gold (`#DAA520`) |
| **Winter** | Ice blue (`#B0E0E6`), white | Navy | Silver (`#C0C0C0`) |

## Typography
- **Font:** Decorative display fonts — Playfair Display (elegant holidays), Dancing Script (romantic), Creepster (Halloween), Mountains of Christmas
- **Font weight:** Bold for impact; varies by occasion
- **Use Google Fonts:** Always provide `font_url` for seasonal display fonts

## Background
- **Type:** Solid (`bg_type: 0`), gradient (`bg_type: 1`), or themed image (`bg_type: 2`)
- **Gradient:** Seasonal transitions — e.g., red → green (Christmas), purple → orange (Halloween)
- **Image:** Seasonal patterns — snowflakes, falling leaves, hearts, confetti
- **Padding:** 12-16px

## Borders & Radius
- **Border:** Can be more decorative than usual — `border_style: 1` or `2`
- **Radius:** Varies; `8px` for friendly, `0px` for bold Black Friday
- **Color:** Thematic — gold border for New Year, green for Christmas

## CTA / Button
- **Style:** Solid (`btn_style: 0` or `2`) with holiday-accent color
- **Text:** Seasonal language — "Shop the Sale", "Get Your Gift", "Treat Yourself", "Jingle & Mingle"
- **Emoji:** Appropriate seasonal emoji encouraged — 🎄, 🎃, ❤️, 🎉

## Coupon/Discount
- **Style:** Festive — e.g., gift-tag style, ornament-colored borders
- **Animation:** Subtle pulse (`animation: 4`) for urgency
- **Text:** Seasonal code names — "XMAS20", "SPOOKY30", "LOVE15"

## When to Use
- Any holiday-specific campaign or seasonal promotion
- User mentions a holiday (Christmas, Halloween, Valentine's, Black Friday, New Year, Easter, Mother's Day, etc.) or season (summer, winter, spring, fall)
- Seasonal clearance sales, holiday gift guides, back-to-school

## When NOT to Use
- Evergreen/general promotions (use `modern_clean` or `bold_urgent`)
- When the holiday is far away (more than 1-2 months)
