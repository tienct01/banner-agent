# Vintage & Retro

## Visual Identity
Warm, nostalgic, and handcrafted. Evokes past decades — 50s diners, 70s record shops, 90s streetwear. Muted, earthy tones and distressed textures. Feels authentic, storied, and human.

## Color Palette
- **Background:** Cream (`#FFFDD0`), warm beige (`#F5E6D3`), faded mustard (`#D4A853`), or muted olive (`#6B705C`)
- **Text:** Dark brown (`#3E2723`), deep burgundy (`#4A1C1C`), or charcoal
- **Accent:** Rust (`#B7410E`), faded denim blue (`#4A6FA5`), terracotta (`#E07A5F`)
- **Avoid:** Neon colors, pure black/white, gradients

## Typography
- **Font:** Serif or display — Playfair Display, Abril Fatface, Bebas Neue, Courier Prime, Permanent Marker
- **Font weight:** Varies; display fonts for headlines, serif for body
- **Font size:** Larger headlines with character; use Google Fonts `font_url`

## Background
- **Type:** Solid color (`bg_type: 0`) or textured image (`bg_type: 2`)
- **Image (optional):** Paper texture, wood grain, old newspaper, grain overlay
- **Gradient:** Avoid gradients; go for flat or textured looks
- **Padding:** Generous (14-18px); creates breathing room

## Borders & Radius
- **Border:** Subtle solid or dashed (`border_style: 1` or `6`) in a muted tone
- **Radius:** Slightly rounded (`4px` or `8px`) or uneven/custom
- **Avoid:** Sharp modern edges, thick borders

## CTA / Button
- **Style:** Solid with rounded corners (`btn_style: 2`) or outlined (`btn_style: 3`)
- **Color:** Warm accent color — rust, olive, denim blue
- **Text:** White or cream on dark buttons; friendly tone — "Discover", "Browse the Collection", "Shop Vintage"

## Coupon/Discount
- **Style:** Solid background with a slightly darker border (`discount_style.border: 0`)
- **Background:** Warm accent tint (e.g., terracotta at 30% opacity)
- **Animation:** None (`animation: 0`); keeps the authentic feel
- **Text:** Classic serif, slightly larger font size

## When to Use
- Vintage clothing, handmade crafts, artisan goods, record stores, bookshops
- User says "vintage", "retro", "old school", "nostalgic", "classic", "handmade"
- Brands with heritage storytelling or artisanal positioning

## When NOT to Use
- Tech/software products (use `cyberpunk_futuristic` or `modern_clean`)
- Urgent flash sales (use `bold_urgent`)
- Corporate/professional services (use `modern_clean`)

## Example
> "Retro banner for our vintage denim collection — cream background, rust-colored text in Playfair Display, 'Shop the Era' CTA."
